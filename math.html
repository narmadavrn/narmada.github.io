Research

  • Convex layer numbers: I am working on improving the best bounds on the layer number of the grid. This is joint work with Travis Dillon at MIT.
  • Circle squaring: This is a project with Spencer Unger to simplify the arguments in this paper. The authors show that any the circle and square of unit measure are equidecomposable with pieces of low Borel complexity and small Minkowski dimension of boundary.
  • Sparse polynomial factorization: This is a project with Shubhangi Saraf to improve the bound on the number of monomials in any factor of a sparse polynomial.
